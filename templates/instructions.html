General Instructions

Welcome to the ECON 3310 Experiment Platform.

You will participate in 10 rounds of simple decision problems. Each round should take about 1 minute or less.

Before the experiment begins, we randomly pick an integer in 1..10, called R. You won’t see it until the end. Your total points = your WEALTH in round R.

In each round you start with 100 points. Choose how much (x) to invest in a risky asset (0–100). A fair coin is flipped: Heads → the investment becomes 2.5x; Tails → it becomes 0. Your wealth that round is 100 − x + 2.5x (heads) or 100 − x (tails).
